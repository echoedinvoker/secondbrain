# Exclamatory sentences

These are sentences that show **shock**, **surprise**, or **strong emotions** and **feelings**.

They end with an **exclamation mark** when in writing.

```py
"I truly don't believe it!"

"How dare you say that!"

"I won the lottery!"

```
