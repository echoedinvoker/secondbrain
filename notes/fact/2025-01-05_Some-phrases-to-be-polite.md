# Some phrases to be polite

```py
"Excuse me!"
# a polite phrase that you say when you do something that could be slightly embarrassing, such as burping or making a noise when you eat

```

```py
"It was lovely to meet you."
# a polite phrase that you say to someone you have just met for the first time, British English

```

```py
* "I'm terribly sorry."
# a polite phrase that you say when you have made a mistake or done something wrong, British English

```

```py
"Could you possibly...?"
# a polite phrase that you use to ask someone to do something, British English
```

```py
"Oh, pardon me!"
# Same as "Excuse me!" but very formal, very British English

```
