# Close questions

Closed questions usually start with an **auxiliary verb**.

> Is / Was / Are / Were / Did / Does / Will / Could

Auxiliary verb + Subject + Verb

```py
"Are you good at English?"

"I'm okay at it."

```

```py
"Did you just eat lunch?"

"Yes, I did."

```
