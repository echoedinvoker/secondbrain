# List of adverbs of frequency

We use *adverbs of frequency* to say **how often** (how many times) something happens.

| Adverb | Frequency |
|--------|-----------|
| Always | 100% |
| Usually | 80% |
| Generally | 70% |
| Sometimes | 50% |
| At times | 50% |
| Occasionally | 30% |
| Rarely | 10% |
| Never | 0% |
