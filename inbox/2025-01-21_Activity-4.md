# Activity 4

Write 2 sentences for both **present simple and present continuous** tense form.

**Present Simple:**
```py
"I go to the gym every day."

"I don't like to eat meat."

```

**Present Continuous:**

```py
"I'm going to the gym right now."

"Where are you living thesedays?"

```
